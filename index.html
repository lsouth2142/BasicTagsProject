Let's go SCUBA diving!

Scuba diving in the Bonne Terre Mines!

One of my favorite spots to go scuba diving in the Mid-West is hands down Bonne Terre Mines. Bonne Terre Mines is
an abbandoned mine that was considered mined out around 30+ years ago. After you walk down in to the mine via the
Mule Entrance, the mine opens up to show just how big it is.

By the time you get down to the water level you feel as though you have just completed a long hike!

Reasons for that are:

  • You have to carry all of your gear in to the mine
  • There are a ton of steps to walk down
  • SCUBA diving gear is heavy
  • The walk down and in to the mine has alot fo rough terrain
